MineWriter Author downloadable books for minecraft!

What is MineWriter?

  • MineWriter is the service where you can write books for minecraft!
  • This site will be used to generate special text files which can be porting into minecraft.
  • In order to port the books you write into minecraft, you need the plugin.

Why use MineWriter

  • You can write extremely large books very easily.
  • Your book is saved and your given a permanent link, never loose your work!
  • You can make unlimited copies of your book.
  • You can make revisions after it's 'published'.

When will it launch?

  • As you can tell, the web development is very early in it's stage
  • The bukkit plugin is also still in development, but it's functional
  • If we get contributors, it will greatly speed up the process
  • Stay in touch for future updates

What is BookDownloader?

  • BookDownloader is the bukkit plugin in which to port plugins from here to the game.
  • While the plugin itself can parse any document, it defaults to only from this site.
  • In order to port the books you write into minecraft, you need the plugin.
